Neonatal Intensive Care

Premature infants lack the capacity to deal with various facets of the real world. Due to preterm birth important body parts like lungs, digestive system, skin and immune system are present in their bodies in an underdeveloped state. A Level IV NICU (Neonatal Intensive Care) at IOT BASED BABY MONITOR INCUBATOR offers the most advanced care for extreme preterm and newly born children with critical health conditions..
